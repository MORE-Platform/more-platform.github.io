Use Case #1

Data Collection for the Active Waiting Study through the MORE Platform

Active Waiting is a…

“quick and ease-to-use intervention concept for filling short breaks and waiting times with spontaneous physical activity”

Andrea Greisberger

FH Campus Wien

The Active Waiting app study

A randomised waitlist control study of physically inactive adults with sedentary jobs following a classic intervention vs control groups design with a participation duration of 1 week.

Data Collection with MORE

De-centralized and remote data collection through the MORE platform.

Daily activity and health-related quality of life self-reports captured.

Use Case #2

Data Collection and Adaptive Intervention in the aktivplan app through the MORE Platform

Aktivplan

Web + mobile app used by healthcare professionals together with patients; enables the joint creation, monitoring and regular review of personalized heart-healthy exercise plans

The SmartPA2 Study

JITAI (Just-in-the-moment Adaptive Intervention) study through aktivplan app that focused on planning / implementation intentions

Aktivplan-MORE Integration

Daily cardiovascular patient exercise adherence captured with possible automated intervention triggering through MORE (via webhooks)
