The Mission

STUDY MANAGER WEB-APP + MOBILE PARTICIPANT-STUDY-COMPANION APP TO IMPLEMENT LIFE-ACCOMPANYING RESEARCH

It can be challenging to collect multimodal research data (e.g. questionnaires, wearable sensor data, etc.) over longer durations and from many study participants. By taking a modular approach, MORE makes it easy to plan and execute situated and longitudinal human-subject research studies.

Our mission with MORE is to streamline and automate study execution and data collection, streamlining procedures between researchers / clinicians and participants.

MODULAR NEAR REAL-TIME SENSOR DATA COLLECTION

Data collected at the start and end of studies can provide valuable insights, but can lack the nuances of ‘in-between’ moments. The possibility of sensor tracking data integration with MORE allows continuous real-time quantitative data to fill in the missing gaps for studies, enabling e.g. sensor-data-based Ecological Momentary Assessment (EMA) studies.

MODULAR

FLEXIBLE AND MODULAR SELF-REPORTED DATA

MORE integrates Limesurvey as a free and open source questionnaires software. This allows researchers to collect self-reported data from their participants in a modular fashion using the full power and flexibility of Limesurvey.

MORE also implements modular informed consent. When first signing in to a study, participants are automatically presented with a customizable informed consent form, including elements based on the specific study configurations.

STUDY ROLE DELEGATION

SAFE AND SECURE DATA COLLECTION

All data are encrypted in transport. Full study structures and data can be downloaded. The MORE app is vetted and available in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

ACCESSIBLE THROUGH MOBILE AND DESKTOP

The MORE ecosystem contains dedicated frontends for researchers and participants. Researchers can plan and manage studies and data collection from the comforts of their lab. Participants on the other hand can engage in daily living, while sensor and questionnaire/survey data is tracked/mediated through the mobile application. Sign-up tokens enable anonymized participation.
