MORE Platform - Digital Health Research

About the Project

The MORE Platform, a 15-month initiative, focuses on creating a sustainable digital infrastructure for health research. Leveraging digital technologies, it addresses challenges like device integration, real-time data collection, participant management, and data security compliance.

The MORE Platform Components

1. Web Application

An innovative web application for professionals to design and manage health studies and evaluations, ensuring streamlined processes and effective participant engagement.

2. Smartphone App

Complementary to the web application, the MORE smartphone app automates study registrations and facilitates data collection through questionnaires and wearable sensors.

Benefits and Implementation

  • Advancing healthcare digitization research.
  • Establishing a sustainable platform for DH innovations.
  • Cultivating future-proof technology for research and development.

Support and Cooperation

This project is bolstered by the Science and Innovation Strategy Salzburg 2025, fostering significant development cooperation in Salzburg's digital health sector.
